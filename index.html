Hybrid Practice Model Improve Skills & Have Fun Playing the Game

Each week, you'll have a Team Practice to work on your team systems & dynamics with your coaches. You'll also have a practice which consists of a Semi-Private Lesson + Loosely Structured Gameplay, where you can work on individual skills, and then have fun implementing them in Games.

These Small Group & Semi-Private Lessons will often be taught by a different coach. Your team's coaches will have input on what needs to be covered, but this gives players the opportunity to hear different perspectives throughout the season.
